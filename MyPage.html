Esports

Esports is competition using video games. It often takes the form of organized, multiplayer video game competitions, particularly between professional players, individually or as teams. Almost every game has an esports scene from Call of Duty to Clash of Clans. Esports have been on the rise the past few years, even some big name celebrities like Drake, have been invested in the industry.
